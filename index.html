What you can do

iKnow your taste

Take a short taste-profile quiz and SakeYō builds your palate. Get bottle recommendations matched to you, each scored for how well it fits.

iiPair with any meal

Start with the dish in front of you and SakeYō suggests the style of sake that works — with the reason why and the words to tell your server.

iiiSee what's on

Browse a calendar of sake tastings, dinners, workshops and brewery tours across Singapore and Japan. Filter, save, and never miss one.

ivFind where to drink it

A little black book of the bars, restaurants and breweries worth seeking out in Japan, by prefecture — plus the places pouring great sake in Singapore.

vKeep a tasting diary

Record every sake you try — brewery, temperature, vessel, notes, pairings, photos and a rating. Watch your taste take shape.

viBuild your cellar

Track the bottles you own, with drinking-window tracking so you know what to open now and what can keep aging.

viiScan a bottle

Point your camera at a label to identify the sake in front of you.

viiiLearn as you go

Start with the seven essentials in Sake 101, decode the label with a plain-English glossary, and read original stories on breweries and regions.

Food pairing

The right pour for what's on the plate.

Tell SakeYō what you're eating and it suggests the style that sings with it — oysters and a crisp junmai, grilled skewers and something with body — plus the reason why and the words to order it.

Tasting diary & cellar

Watch your palate take shape.

Log every bottle — brewery, temperature, vessel, notes and a rating — and SakeYō turns it into a picture of what you love. Keep your cellar in order, with drinking windows so you know what to open now.
