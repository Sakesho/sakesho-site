Privacy Policy

Your privacy.

Last updated: 9 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Sakesho PTE LTD ("we", "us", "our"), the company behind the SakeYō app, collects, uses, discloses and protects your personal data when you use the SakeYō mobile application and related services (the "Service"). We are committed to handling your personal data in accordance with the Singapore Personal Data Protection Act 2012 (PDPA) and other applicable laws.

By creating an account or using the Service, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Who this applies to

SakeYō relates to alcoholic beverages and is intended only for users who are of legal drinking age in their country of residence. The Service is not directed to children, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under the legal drinking age.

Information we collect

Information you provide

  • Account details — your email address and any name or display name you choose when you sign up.
  • Taste profile — your answers to the taste-preference quiz used to personalise recommendations.
  • Your content — diary entries, ratings, tasting notes, food pairings, cellar bottles, wishlists, saved events and any photos you choose to add.
  • Communications — messages you send us, such as support requests and feedback.

Information collected automatically

  • Usage and device data — basic information such as app version, device model, operating system and diagnostic or crash information, used to keep the app working reliably.

Device permissions

  • Camera — used only when you choose to scan a bottle label.
  • Photo library — used only when you choose to attach a photo to a diary entry.
  • Notifications — if you allow them, used to send relevant alerts. You can turn these off at any time in your device settings.

We do not access your camera or photos in the background, and we do not collect precise location data.

How we use your information

  • To create and manage your account and keep you signed in.
  • To personalise your recommendations, taste profile and pairings.
  • To store and display your diary, cellar, wishlists and saved events.
  • To operate, maintain, secure and improve the Service.
  • To respond to your support requests and communicate with you about the Service.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

How your information is stored and shared

We do not sell your personal data. We share it only with service providers that help us run the Service, and only as needed to provide it:

  • Supabase — provides our authentication, database and storage infrastructure, which holds your account and content.
  • Hosting and infrastructure providers — host our backend services and this website.

These providers process data on our behalf under agreements that require appropriate safeguards. We may also disclose information where required by law, to enforce our terms, or to protect the rights, safety and security of our users and the Service. Because these providers may operate outside Singapore, your data may be transferred to and stored in other countries; where this happens, we take steps to ensure a comparable standard of protection as required by the PDPA.

Data retention

We keep your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service. When you delete your account, we permanently delete or anonymise your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it to meet legal obligations.

Your rights and choices

  • Access and correction — you can view and edit much of your information directly in the app, and you may request a copy or correction of your personal data.
  • Account deletion — you can delete your account and associated data at any time from Settings → Account in the app, or by emailing us (see below).
  • Withdraw consent — you may withdraw consent to our processing of your personal data, subject to legal or contractual restrictions and reasonable notice.

To exercise any of these rights, email support@sakesho.app from your account email address. We may need to verify your identity before acting on a request.

Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical and physical safeguards designed to protect your personal data against loss, misuse and unauthorised access. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, however, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children

The Service is not intended for, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from, anyone below the legal drinking age. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above and, where appropriate, notify you in the app. Your continued use of the Service after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact us
